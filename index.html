Instructions

1 - Place your ships! Left clicking on your board places a ship, right clicking changes orientation (horizontal/vertical)

3 - After your attack, it's the computer's turn. Once it is done, you can attack again!

4 - The game is won when one player has sunk all the opponent's ships!
